About AI ROI Calculator

Created by an 18-year-old indie builder, July 2025

AI tools are powerful, but not always worth the cost. This calculator helps you see if you're actually saving time or earning more than you're spending.

I built this tool as a way to help creators, freelancers, and solo builders make smarter decisions in a world full of $29/month subscriptions. There's no login, no tracking, and no gimmicks — just math.

I plan to expand this site with blogs, comparisons, and ROI breakdowns for every major AI tool on the market. Stay tuned.
